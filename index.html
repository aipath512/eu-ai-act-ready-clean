Frequently Asked Questions

Does the EU AI Act apply to every company using AI?

The obligations depend on the role of the organization, the AI system used and the applicable requirements under Regulation (EU) 2024/1689.

What is the first step toward EU AI Act readiness?

The first step is identifying AI systems, their purpose, responsible persons, data usage and risk classification.

What does Article 4 require?

Article 4 requires providers and deployers to take measures ensuring sufficient AI literacy for people using AI systems.

What can be generated for my firm?

The platform can generate structured readiness documentation, governance materials, inventories and evidence preparation structures.

Does documentation replace legal advice?

No. Documentation supports operational readiness and evidence management but does not replace legal or technical advice.
