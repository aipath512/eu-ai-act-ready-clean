Compliance FAQ

What is the EU AI Act?

The EU AI Act is the first comprehensive legal framework for AI, ensuring safety and fundamental rights.

Who needs to comply?

All providers and deployers of AI systems operating within the EU or affecting EU citizens must comply.

What are GPAI obligations?

General Purpose AI providers must ensure transparency, technical documentation, and copyright compliance.

What is the role of the AI Office?

The AI Office oversees GPAI models and enforces uniform implementation across the European Union.

How to classify high-risk AI?

Classification is based on the intended purpose and the potential impact on safety and fundamental rights.

What is AI Literacy?

Article 4 requires organizations to ensure staff have sufficient knowledge of AI systems and their risks.

How to create an AI inventory?

List all deployed AI systems, their risk categories, and the operational data they consume.

What are transparency requirements?

Systems must clearly disclose that they are AI-generated and provide readable instructions for use.

What are the penalties?

Fines can reach up to 35 million EUR or 7% of total worldwide annual turnover for severe violations.

How to start readiness?

Start with an AI inventory, risk assessment, and technical documentation mapping as per EU AI Act Ready™ guidelines.
