Frequently Asked Questions

Does the EU AI Act apply to every company using AI?

The applicable obligations depend on the role of the organization, the AI system involved and the requirements of Regulation (EU) 2024/1689.

What is the first step toward AI Act readiness?

The first step is identifying AI systems, their purpose, responsibilities, data usage and possible risk classification.

What does Article 4 require?

Article 4 requires providers and deployers to take measures ensuring sufficient AI literacy for people using AI systems.

What can YOUR FIRM generate?

The generator can create structured readiness documentation adapted to an organization's parameters and governance needs.

Does this replace legal advice?

No. The platform supports operational readiness, documentation and evidence preparation. It does not replace legal or technical advice.
