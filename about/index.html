About EU-112.

An independent DIY toolkit by AiVenture S.R.L. — helping businesses get EU AI Act-ready, themselves.

EU-112 is a free do-it-yourself toolkit that helps businesses make their websites and AI systems ready for the EU AI Act — on their own terms, before the Article 50 enforcement date of August 2, 2026. No consultant. No fee. Just the tools.

It is an independent educational initiative by AiVenture S.R.L., the company behind 5thElement.ai and the AI Delivery Infrastructure (ADI) doctrine.

Why “112”?

112 is the single emergency number recognized across all 27 EU member states. We use it metaphorically — to signal urgency around the compliance deadline, not to offer an emergency service.

// Independent initiative — read this EU-112 is an independent educational initiative by AiVenture S.R.L. (CUI 51415878). It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the official 112 emergency service or any EU institution. The number 112 is used metaphorically. This toolkit supports EU AI Act readiness; it does not satisfy or certify compliance, and it is not legal advice. Legal compliance remains the responsibility of the deployer.
