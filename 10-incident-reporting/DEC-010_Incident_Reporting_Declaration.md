# 1. Purpose

This declaration confirms that {{COMPANY_NAME}} has established, implemented and maintains a documented Incident Reporting process for Artificial Intelligence systems in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 (EU AI Act).

The declaration demonstrates management commitment to ensuring that AI-related incidents are identified, reported, investigated, documented and resolved in a consistent and traceable manner.

---

# 2. Declaration

{{COMPANY_NAME}} declares that:

- AI-related incidents are identified and reported without undue delay;
- every reported incident is documented and assigned a unique Incident ID;
- incident investigations include root cause analysis;
- corrective and preventive actions are implemented where necessary;
- objective evidence supporting each incident is maintained;
- incident records are available for internal audits and regulatory inspections.

---

# 3. Management Commitment

Management commits to:

- maintain an effective AI Incident Reporting process;
- allocate sufficient resources for incident investigation;
- ensure timely implementation of corrective actions;
- periodically review incident trends;
- continuously improve AI governance based on lessons learned;
- support regulatory inspections and external audits.

---

# 4. Scope

This declaration applies to:

- internally developed AI systems;
- externally acquired AI systems;
- proof-of-concept AI systems;
- production AI systems;
- AI infrastructure;
- all personnel using AI systems on behalf of {{COMPANY_NAME}}.

---

# 5. Review Frequency

Incident Reporting arrangements shall be reviewed:

- annually;
- after every significant AI-related incident;
- after major AI system changes;
- after regulatory updates;
- before regulatory inspections.
