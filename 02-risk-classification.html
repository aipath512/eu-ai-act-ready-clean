Module 02 — Risk Classification

The purpose of this policy is to establish the principles, responsibilities and requirements for the classification of Artificial Intelligence systems in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 (EU AI Act). This policy ensures that every AI system used, developed, integrated or deployed by your organization is consistently assessed, classified and documented according to its regulatory risk category.
